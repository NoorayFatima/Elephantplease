Before the Rental

Find an item: Search for the desired item and filter by
category and location.

Request and wait: Send a request to the lender and
wait for the lender to confirm your request.

Arrange handover:Use the chat function to arrange a convenient time.

During the Rental

Enjoy your rental: Make the most of your time with the item. Have fun!

Take good care: Use and treat the item as you would if it was your own.

Return on time:To avoid surcharges and penalties, return the item on time. Message the lender and request if you would like the item for a few more days.

After the Rental

Rate the lender: Take this opportunity and review the lender for other members of the community.

Repeat: Search and rent again!

Play your Part

Renting on Elephant Please isn't just convenient and cost-saving. By purchasing less and renting more, you're also contributing to a circular economy, making better use of resources and helping to protect this wonderful planet we call home.
