Before Lending

List an item: Look around your home and Fill out a few fields to describe your item.

Update availability: Remember to update the dates your item will not be available to allow users to have a better understanding of the availability.

Respond promptly: Answer queries users may have to increase the likelihood of a rental.

When Lending

Meet the User: Arrange to meet the user to handover the item.

Keep in touch: Arrange in advance for the return of the item. Contact Elephant Please if there are any issues.

Retrieve the item:To avoid surcharges and penalties, return the item on time. Message the lender and request if you would like the item for a few more days.

After lending

Rate the User: Take this opportunity and review the lender for other members of the community.

Repeat: Search and rent again!

Play your Part

Renting on Elephant Please isn't just convenient and cost-saving. By purchasing less and renting more, you're also contributing to a circular economy, making better use of resources and helping to protect this wonderful planet we call home.
